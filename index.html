🌟 Immediate Stress Relief

You're experiencing stress right now, and that's okay. Try these quick techniques:

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Box Breathing: Inhale 4 counts → Hold 4 → Exhale 4 → Hold 4
  • Progressive Relaxation: Tense and release each muscle group
  • Positive Affirmation: "This feeling is temporary, and I can handle this"
